About Us

Created by students at Oakland Technical High School

Who Are We?

We are a group of passionate students from Oakland Technical High School who love exploring nature and building digital tools to help others. Our website, Hike Map, was created to connect people with real-time hiking conditions in California’s breathtaking National Parks.

Why We Built Hike Map

Hiking is something that we all enjoy, and it has brought us all closer to nature and each other. We wanted to build a space where people can share and access up-to-date trail conditions, experiences, and safety tips — making outdoor adventures more accessible and sustainable for everyone.
